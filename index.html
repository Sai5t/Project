Frequently Asked Questions

Why don't my Google Ads conversions match GA4 exactly?
Differences in attribution models, counting methods, and cookie settings often cause minor data discrepancies. I clean up tag setups to align them as closely as possible.
What is Enhanced Conversion tracking?
It safely passes hashed user-provided data (like email addresses) back to Google, improving performance tracking accuracy when traditional cookie paths fail.
